What Is Tech Enthusiast of the Month?


Welcome to the Tech Enthusiast of the Month, where we celebrate the extraordinary individuals who are shaping the world of technology through their unwavering dedication and hard work.


In a fast-paced industry that is constantly evolving, these remarkable individuals stand out as beacons of innovation, pushing boundaries and making a difference with their remarkable efforts and commitment.


Join us as we shine a spotlight on the true heroes of the tech world - the Tech Enthusiast of the Month.

Current Tech Enthusiast of the Month - November 2023

Saima Uddin


Course Lead for PA's Women in Tech Intro to Web Dev coding course!


Saima has been a software engineer at PA Consulting for 3 years. Saima (along with other fantastic instructors) runs an Intro to Web Dev coding course; which has helped so many women feel confident to learn a new skill. We celebrate the commitment and hardwork shown!!
